JS3 Simple Validator

Requirements

  1. Prompt the user to enter their phone number with dashes
  2. according to the simple rule that the fourth and eighth characters must be dashes
  3. birth date: must follow the format xx/xx/xx
  4. postal code: must follow the format xxxxx OR xxxxx-xxxx
  5. state abbreviation: must be two characters, must be all caps
  6. married: must be yes or no, may be any capitalization: YES, Yes, yes
